01

What Are Cookies?

Cookies and localStorage are small pieces of data stored in your browser. They help the website remember your preferences (language, theme) and keep the Tsunonton experience smooth.

02

What We Use

  • Theme preference - light/dark mode (saved in localStorage)
  • Language choice - so the site stays in your selected language
  • Session data - minimal technical info to make navigation and game features work correctly
We do NOT use: advertising cookies, tracking pixels, Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any third-party profiling tools.
03

Essential Storage Only

All storage we use is strictly necessary for the website and Telegram mini-app to function. Without it some features (theme switch, language, game state) will not work properly.

Tsun Points and game progress are stored server-side and linked only to your Telegram ID - not to cookies.

04

How to Manage or Delete Cookies

You can clear cookies and localStorage at any time in your browser settings:

  • Chrome - Settings - Privacy and security - Clear browsing data
  • Firefox / Safari / Edge - similar options

Blocking cookies may make the site less comfortable, but you can still read all information and use the Telegram bot.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when the site or game changes. Any material update will be announced in @tsunonton_chat.
