Open Card Packs

Collect daily resources and open Card Scrolls to discover Tsundere Waifu cards. Each scroll reveals a random card - from Common to Legendary - with provably fair randomness.

Fuse & Upgrade

Two crafting paths: the safe route (collect 10 identical cards for a guaranteed upgrade) or the risky route (combine five cards of the same rarity for a chance at the tier above). The ultimate Legend requires both strategy and patience.
