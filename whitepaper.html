00 - Abstract

Abstract

$TSUN is a community-originated GameFi token deployed on the TON Blockchain. It combines an anime tsundere cultural identity with a practical GameFi engagement loop centered around a Telegram Mini-App clicker game, leaderboard competition, and a phased token airdrop program.

The project is designed around three core principles: transparency (renounced contract, locked liquidity, all parameters publicly verifiable on-chain), accessibility (free-to-play, no investment required to participate in the game), and community ownership (no VC backing, no presale, no team token cliff without disclosed vesting).

This document describes the project technical architecture, token economics, game mechanics, roadmap, and governance philosophy. It is intended for informational purposes and does not constitute financial or investment advice.

02 - Problem Statement

Problem Statement

The GameFi Engagement Problem

The majority of GameFi projects suffer from short-lived engagement cycles. Players join to earn, earn until yields decline, then leave. This creates a destructive cycle of speculative inflow followed by mass exodus, destroying token value and community cohesion.

The Barrier Problem

Most GameFi titles require users to first purchase tokens or NFTs before playing, creating a financial barrier that filters out community-first participants in favor of purely speculative actors. This misaligns incentives from day one.

The Distribution Problem

Token launches disproportionately favor insiders: venture capitalists receive discounted allocations, team wallets vest silently, and early access is sold rather than earned. Retail participants enter at a structural disadvantage.

The Platform Fragmentation Problem

Most GameFi ecosystems require players to navigate multiple platforms: a separate game client, a browser extension wallet, a DEX, and a community forum. Each additional step loses users. The friction compounds at every handoff.

03 - The $TSUN Solution

The $TSUN Solution

Free-to-Play First

The Tsunonton clicker game is entirely free to play. No token purchase is required to participate, earn Tsun Points, climb the leaderboard, or qualify for the airdrop. This inverts the standard GameFi model: play first, earn later, hold if you choose.

Telegram-Native

By building entirely within the Telegram Mini-App ecosystem, $TSUN eliminates the platform fragmentation problem. Players already use Telegram daily. The game, the bot, the community, and the wallet (via @wallet or Tonkeeper) all live within a single environment. This reduces onboarding friction to near zero.

Merit-Based Distribution

The Phase 3 airdrop distributes tokens based on leaderboard performance, a direct proxy for genuine engagement. This rewards players who actually participated in building the community, not wallets that were funded at launch.

Transparent Economics

Every aspect of $TSUN token economics is publicly verifiable on-chain. The contract is renounced, the liquidity pool is locked, and there are zero taxes on buys or sells. There are no hidden wallets and no backdoors.

04 - Technology

Technology Stack

TON Blockchain

$TSUN is deployed on The Open Network (TON), a layer-1 blockchain originally developed by Telegram and now maintained as an open-source project by the TON Foundation. TON is the natural choice for $TSUN for several reasons:

  • Native Telegram integration - TON is the only blockchain with a first-party integration into Telegram via TON Connect, @wallet, and the Mini-App ecosystem, giving $TSUN direct access to Telegram user base.
  • High throughput - TON sharding architecture supports millions of transactions per second, ensuring the game and token mechanics scale without congestion.
  • Low fees - transaction costs on TON are a fraction of a cent, making micro-interactions economically viable for all participants.
  • Jetton standard - $TSUN is implemented as a Jetton, ensuring compatibility with TON-native wallets, DEXes, and explorers.

Smart Contract

The $TSUN token contract is a standard Jetton implementation deployed on the TON mainnet. The contract has been renounced, ownership has been transferred to a null address, permanently removing any ability for the team to modify the contract, mint additional tokens, or execute admin functions.

Telegram Mini-App

The Tsunonton clicker game is built as a Telegram Mini-App, a web application embedded directly within the Telegram interface via the Mini-App API. Game state is persisted server-side, linked to the user Telegram ID.

TON Connect

05 - Game Mechanics

Tsunonton Clicker - Game Mechanics

The Tsunonton clicker is a Telegram Mini-App game accessible via @tsunonton_bot. It is free to play, requires no wallet, and no token purchase. The game is the primary engagement and community-building mechanism of the project.

Core Loop

Tap and Earn
Tap the Tsun-chan mascot to earn Tsun Points. Each tap generates points based on your current Tap Power stat.
Energy System
Tapping consumes Energy, which regenerates over time. Energy capacity and regen rate can be upgraded.
Upgrades
Spend Tsun Points to upgrade Tap Power, Energy Capacity, Regeneration Rate, and Passive Income.
Quests
Daily and weekly quests provide point bonuses and boost leaderboard position before Phase 3 snapshot.

Leaderboard and Ranking

All players are ranked globally by total Tsun Points accumulated. The leaderboard is the primary competitive mechanic and the basis for Phase 3 airdrop eligibility. Leaderboard rankings will be snapshotted at the close of Phase 2.

Tsun Points - Important Clarification

Tsun Points are in-game items only. They have no monetary value, cannot be traded or transferred, and are not $TSUN tokens. They exist solely within the game environment as a leaderboard ranking mechanism. Airdrop eligibility is determined by leaderboard position, not by any direct conversion of Tsun Points to tokens.

09 - Security

Security and Trust Architecture

Renounced Contract

The $TSUN smart contract ownership has been permanently transferred to a null address on the TON Blockchain. This irreversibly removes any ability for the project team to modify the contract code, enable transaction taxes, mint additional tokens, pause transfers, or execute any privileged administrative function. The contract's behaviour is fixed at deployment and independently verifiable on Tonviewer.

Locked Liquidity

The LP tokens representing the initial $TSUN/TON liquidity deposited on STON.fi have been locked via TonRaffles, a TON-native liquidity locking service. This prevents the withdrawal of the liquidity pool - commonly known as a rug pull. Lock status and expiry are publicly verifiable on-chain at any time.

Zero Tax Architecture

$TSUN has a 0% buy and 0% sell tax, hardcoded at deployment. No redistribution fees, no development wallet taxes, and no hidden transaction costs. Because the contract is renounced, these parameters cannot be changed by any party.

Fixed Supply

The total supply of 1,000,000,000 $TSUN is permanently fixed. Renouncement eliminates any mint authority. There are no inflationary emissions and no mechanisms to increase supply under any circumstance.

Transparency Commitment

All significant project wallets - including the Game Rewards allocation and the Community allocation - will be publicly documented and linked from the project documentation. Community members are encouraged to monitor wallet activity on Tonviewer and flag any unexpected movements for community review.

10 - Team

Team

$TSUN is built by an anonymous indie development team. Anonymity is a deliberate choice consistent with the decentralised, community-owned ethos of the project - and is standard practice across the TON meme and GameFi token space.

Why anonymity is not a risk here

In traditional finance, team identity is used as a proxy for accountability. In a renounced, locked, zero-tax token on a public blockchain, the code itself provides that accountability. Every safety parameter is verifiable on-chain without requiring trust in any individual:

  • The renounced contract means no team member can change the rules.
  • The locked LP means no team member can drain liquidity.
  • The fixed supply means no team member can inflate the supply.
  • The 0% tax means no team member earns from your trades.
Code over credentials. These are facts, not promises - and they hold regardless of who built them. Verify each one independently on Tonviewer and TonRaffles before forming a judgment.

Team composition

The team covers smart contract development, Telegram Mini-App game development, web frontend engineering, community management, and content creation. Team members operate under pseudonyms within the community and are accessible through official Telegram channels.

Vesting as accountability

The 18.3% Dev and Marketing allocation is subject to a 6-month cliff followed by an 18-month linear vesting schedule. This structure means the team has a long-term financial incentive aligned with the project's success - not a fast-exit structure.
